ASSESSMENT GUIDELINES

No. Vehicle Assessment Assessment Guidelines for Line Manager
1 Is the Vehicle Tyre Pressure correct? Front Y/N ; Rear Y/N Instruct the employee to check tyre pressure at a fuel station and ensure the air is filled according to the manufacturerâ€™s instructions.
2 How is the threading condition of the tyres? Are there any cuts? Check the tyre thoroughly by inspecting the tread depth. Spin the wheel slowly and check for cuts, splits, bulges, or worn fabric.
3 Is the speedometer functioning properly? Test ride the vehicle from Point A to Point B and check if the speedometer is functioning. The distance covered should reflect in the meter reading & the speedo needle should indicate the speed of the vehicle.
4 Is the fuel meter functioning properly? Turn the ignition on and observe the needle on the fuel gauge, check if it moves. The reading of the fuel meter should match the fuel present in the vehicle.
5 Are both mirrors present? Is it positioned properly as per rider's eye line? Check physically if both the mirrors are present. The mirrors should be accurately aligned to the eye line of the rider while sitting in riding position.
6 Do the front & rear brakes function properly? Test ride the vehicle and check if the brakes are effective when applied. Brake levers should not be too soft when applied.
7 Is the horn functioning properly? Gently press the horn button and check if the horn is functioning properly. The sound output should be clear and audible. Highlight if the horn is extremely loud or musical.
8 Is the auto/ kick starter functioning properly? Gently press the auto start button and check if the engine switches on in a smooth manner. Use the kicker lever if the auto starter is not present.
9 Is the vehicle key in good condition? Check for any bend or rust Physically check the teeth condition of the key, highlight if it's worn out. Check for any bend or rust in the key.
10 How is the seat condition of the vehicle? Is there any damage/cuts? Check the seat condition by gently pressing the seat, highlight if the seat is hard or worn out. Seat assembly should be soft and comfortable to avoid back aches of a rider. Check for any cuts or damages.
11 Is the main stand & side stand functioning properly? Use the main & side stands, operation should be smooth and easy. Check for any bend or rust in the stands.
12 Are the number plates of the vehicle clearly visible? All the characters in the number plates should be clear and visible. It is illegal to use illegible or stylish fonts. Govt has recommended to use IND(embossed)number plates.
